Priviti Cafe

I had visited in Dec 2018.

And its very good.Juhu's Prithivi Cafe is one of the most iconic and happening cafes in Mumbai. It is located alongside the legendary Prithvi Theatre, which also has a fun little book shop and gallery. The decor of this place is pretty unique; with posters of Hindi Cinema's classics. Stuffed Parathas & Chhole, Amritsari Kulche, Rajma Chawal, Pav Bhaji, Pizzas, Iced Tea, Irish Coffee and Cutting Chai and wide range of delectable desserts are some of the most famous items on the menu.
